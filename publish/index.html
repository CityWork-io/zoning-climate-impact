Data Notes ▾

Gasoline estimates derive from CNT's 2022 Housing + Transportation Index, converted from modeled vehicle-miles traveled at 24 mpg, an approximate on-road light-duty fleet average from FHWA Highway Statistics. Block-group values are aggregated to H3 hexagons at three resolutions for display. Region Snapshot statistics are population-weighted across raw block groups so they reflect the typical resident, not the typical hexagon. Block groups with fewer than 100 residents are excluded.

Income from ACS 2020–2024 5-year estimates at the block-group level. Where the Census Bureau suppresses block-group income, tract or county estimates are substituted. Named block groups verified against ACS margins of error.

Job accessibility from EPA Smart Location Database V3, computed from 2020 GTFS transit schedules and LODES employment data.

Gas prices use the AAA Virginia regular average of $4.245/gallon as of late May 2026. Year-ago comparison uses the AAA Virginia average from the same period in 2025 ($2.987). Data vintages vary by source.
